Important Notice

ACRRM is currently experiencing a high volume of AST applications. To ensure quality training opportunities align with community needs, we are prioritising the assessment of applications based on current training and workforce priorities. This may cause delays in the review of some new applications. We apologise for any inconvenience and appreciate your understanding.

Third party accredited sites endorsed by ACRRM will not be listed in the search results. Accreditation information for these sites can be accessed via the following websites:

  • AST Anaesthesia ANZCA
  • AST Obstetrics and Gynaecology RANZCOG
  • PMC Postgraduate Medical Councils

If you have any questions, please contact the Post Accreditation Team at postaccreditation@acrrm.org.au or Isabel Broderick at i.broderick@acrrm.org.au .
